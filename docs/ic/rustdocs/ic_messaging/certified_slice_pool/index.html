[−][src]Module ic_messaging::certified_slice_pool

A pool of incoming CertifiedStreamSlices used by XNetPayloadBuilderImpl to build XNetPayloads without the need for I/O on the critical path.

Structs

CertifiedSlicePool

A pool of CertifiedStreamSlices that provides support for taking out valid certified sub-slices of arbitrary size starting from arbitrary stream indices; and maintains cached stream positions, so an asynchronous process may populate the pool in the background with appropriate slices.

UnpackedStreamSlice

An unpacked CertifiedStreamSlice: a slice of the stream of messages produced by a subnet together with a cryptographic proof that the majority of that subnet agrees on it.
